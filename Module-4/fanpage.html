Carrie Underwood's Impact

Carrie's career took off after winning American Idol in 2005. I have enjoyed listening to her albums, and some songs are very powerful for me. The most influential album for me, personally, was her Cry Pretty album. I repeatedly listened to "The Bullet" and had my own 'therapeutic cry session.' The album isn't restricted to emotional songs, though. If you need a 'pick me-up,' try the song Southbound.
